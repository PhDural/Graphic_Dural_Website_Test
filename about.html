PHILIP DURAL

Philip Dural is the founder of Graphic Dural LLC. His passion for the design profession is fueled by a tenacious inclination he has towards the research of various methods of design, the application of those methods, their historical development, and the subjects represented in a project.

The foundation of his work is Jay Hambidge’s theory on Dynamic Symmetries, a proportioning system and design methodology Hambidge articulated in the 1920s. The system uses dynamic rectangles, including root rectangles based on ratios such as √2, √3, √5, the golden ratio, its square root, and its square.

The influence of Hambidge is evident throughout Philip's body of work and demonstrates the results of his dedication to pursuits in scholarship.
